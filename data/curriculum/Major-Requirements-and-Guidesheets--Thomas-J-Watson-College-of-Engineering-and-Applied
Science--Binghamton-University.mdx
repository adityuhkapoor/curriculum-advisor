#  Major Requirements and Guidesheets

These suggested course tracks are **based on the year of entry** of a student.
These are only suggestions, [ refer to the University Academic Guide for the
official requirements ](https://catalog.binghamton.edu/) for each major.

For official archived requirements refer to the [ University Academic Guide
](/bulletin/2023-24/archives.html "BU Bulletin Archive") . Select the desired
year, then click "Digital archive access" under the heading View Online.

For more information on [ graduate-level requirements go here
](/watson/student-services/advising/graduate.html) .

Take note of all Pre- or Co-requisites
